Action Hold 'Em Rules

How to Play

Goal: Be the first player to reach the target score. Once a player reaches the target score, the current hand becomes the final hand. After that hand is scored, the highest total score wins.

Starting a Hand

  • Each player starts with 2 cards.
  • No community cards are revealed yet.
  • The game begins in the Pre-Flop phase.

Your Turn

  1. Draw from either the Deck or the Discard Pile.
  2. Discard one card from your hand.

You may discard a normal card or play an Action Card. Your turn ends immediately after discarding.

Community Cards

  • Flop: 3 community cards are revealed.
  • Turn: 1 additional community card is revealed.
  • River: 1 final community card is revealed.
  • Showdown: All hands are scored automatically.

Building Your Hand

  • Use your hand cards and the community cards.
  • Only 5 cards may be used to create a scoring hand.

Important Rules

  • Action Cards cannot appear in the community cards.
  • Wild Cards cannot appear in the community cards.
  • Action Cards cannot be drawn from the discard pile.
  • If you draw from the discard pile, you cannot immediately discard that same card.
  • Skip causes a player to lose their next turn.
  • Face Up forces a player's hand to remain visible.

End of Hand

After the River round, all hands are scored, action card penalties are applied, bonuses are awarded, and scores are added to the scoreboard.

Scoring

  • Natural Royal Flush = 500
  • Royal Flush = 350
  • Natural Straight Flush = 300
  • Straight Flush = 225
  • Natural Four of a Kind = 200
  • Four of a Kind = 150
  • Full House = 125
  • Flush = 100
  • Straight = 80
  • Three of a Kind = 40
  • Two Pair = 30
  • One Pair = -25
  • High Card = -50

Best Hand Bonus

Best hand gets +30. Split among ties. No bonus if 4+ players tie.

Action Cards

  • Skip: Target loses next turn.
  • Trade Hands: Swap hands with target.
  • Pass Left / Pass Right: Rotate all hands.
  • Burn: Target loses a random card.
  • Burn All: All opponents lose a random card.
  • Bonus: Draw an extra card.
  • Full Reset: Burn hand and draw 2 new cards.
  • Face Up: Hand remains visible.
  • Peek: View another player's hand.
  • Change Community: Replace a community card.

Wild Cards

  • True Wild = Any card.
  • Suit Wild = Any card of the matching suit.
